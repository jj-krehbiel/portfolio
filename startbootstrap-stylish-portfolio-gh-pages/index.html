About Me

My Perspective

I am a Georgia Tech-certified data analyst with a thirst for solving complex puzzles for businesses, nonprofits, and civic groups. Long before studying data science, I immersed myself in data journalism websites, like FiveThirtyEight, to better understand current events through a quantitative lens, which partly led me to pursuing this exciting field. With a background in the social sciences and a personal passion for improvisational theater, I intertwine my knack for storytelling and my sociological viewpoint to present compelling, statistically reliable analysis from raw data. I am a self-motivated team player who can successfully collaborate remotely. My approach to new challenges is with patience, curiosity, and an eagerness to continually learn.
